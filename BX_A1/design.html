      The Website template in Boxed-Width Design. Boxed-Width is when your content has a visible frame to the left and right side of the screen. Provides a fixed width for the content. This allows the content display and position to remain relatively the same across different monitor sizes and resolutions, so you don’t have worry about different people having different visual experiences
